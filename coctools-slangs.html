CoC Glossary
This page gives you quick access to common Clash of Clans terms and slang to better understand gameplay and strategies.

General Terms

FC
- Friendly Challenge.

Mirror
- war term used to refer to the opposing war base with the same base number as the player.

Swag
- A term used to refer to the action of either not using certain troops and/or spells.

Kiting
- A technique where you place a troop to lure friendly or enemy troops to a specific location. For example, placing a troop to lure your Warden exactly where you want it to go.

Troop Terms

Coco Loons
- A Balloon deployed as a sacrifice in order to trigger Seeking Air Mines.

Hybrid
- Combination of Hog Riders and Miners.

Hydra
- Combination of Dragons and Dragon riders.

Lavaloon
- Uses Lava Hounds to tank defenses while Balloons follow behind.

FF
- Abbreviation for Flame Flinger.

Big Boy
- A Subtroop spawned by the Super Witch that resembles a large Skeleton.

Queen Charge (QC)
- uses the Archer Queen along with Healers to take out key defenses deep into the base.

Queen Walk (QW)
uses the Archer Queen along with Healers to take out a portion "OUTSIDE" base for funneling purposes.

RC Charge (Spirit Walk)
- Combines the Invisibility spell with the Royal Champion, letting her charge deep into the base.

Warden Walk (Fireball)
- Using the Wardens Fireball to destroy clustered defenses while being supported by Healers and spells.

Attack Strategies

Funneling
- is the process of taking out key buildings so that your troops will be directed towards the core of the base (such as using QC on the left and siege barracks on the right.)

Smash Attacks
- Focuses on sending high hitpoint troops into the middle of the base and rely heavily on the Grand Warden’s ability.

Hero Dive Attacks (SUI)
- Uses the Log Launcher to open the path for your heroes, sending them deep into the base.

Spam Attacks
- A straightforward strategy where large numbers of troops are deployed quickly with minimal funneling

Skelly/Bat Donut Attack
- Uses Skeleton or Bat Spells with Invisibility to destroy key defenses, usually the Clan Castle.

Blizzard Attacks
- Uses the Battle Blimp carrying Super Wizards inside, supported by well timed Invisibility spells to wipe out the core of the base.

Fireball Attacks
-Using the Wardens Fireball equipment to destroy clustered defenses while being supported by Healers and spells.

Sniping
- The act of destroying Buildings from a distance without retaliation (such as Archers placed barely out of range of a defense)

Base Terminology

Island A defense surrounded by nothing and then a layer of the wall in all directions.

Backend A part of the base at the end of an attack.

Trash Buildings
- Non-defenses that are placed on the outside. (Collectors, Barracks, etc.)

Core
- The center of a base or the heaviest defenses stacked

Tesla Farm
- A pack of tesla popping up clustered

Troll Tesla
- A Tesla in the corner, mainly used to create time fails.

Trophy Base
- A base used to minimise trophy loss. Resources aren't a big concern of trophy bases

Farm Base
- Focus on protecting resources by placing storages in well-defended compartments

Hybrid Base
- A Mix of war and farming strategies, aiming to protect resources while maintaining a strong defensive core.

War Base
- A base used to minimise trophy loss. Resources aren't a big concern of trophy bases
